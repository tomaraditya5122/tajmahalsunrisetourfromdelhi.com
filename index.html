Tour Overview

The Taj Mahal Sunrise Tour from Delhi is a magical journey designed for travelers who want to witness the breathtaking beauty of the Taj Mahal at sunrise, when the first rays of the sun cast a golden glow on its white marble surface. This same-day tour by comfortable AC car begins early in the morning and offers a peaceful, crowd-free experience of one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Departing from Delhi around 2:30 AM, you'll reach Agra before sunrise and be guided through the Taj Mahal just as the sun rises behind it ‚Äì a moment photographers and romantics dream of. The tour continues with a visit to the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site, followed by breakfast at a 5-star hotel. Later, explore Mehtab Bagh (optional) and local markets before heading back to Delhi in the evening.

With door-to-door transport, a professional guide, and smooth itinerary, this tour offers a perfect blend of history, beauty, and convenience for a memorable day trip to Agra.

Daily Itinerary

2:30 AM

Pickup from Delhi/NCR

Your private driver will pick you up in an AC car for a comfortable journey to Agra via Yamuna Expressway (approx. 3.5 hours).

5:45 AM

Arrive at Taj Mahal before Sunrise

Meet your guide and enter the Taj Mahal as the sun rises, watching the monument change color in the morning light. Your guide will share fascinating stories of Shah Jahan and Mumtaz.

8:00 AM

Breakfast at 5-Star Hotel

Enjoy a delicious buffet breakfast at a luxurious hotel such as ITC Mughal or Courtyard Marriott.

9:00 AM

Visit Agra Fort

Explore the impressive Agra Fort, where emperors once lived and where Shah Jahan was imprisoned by his own son. See beautiful courtyards, palaces, and views of the Taj Mahal from its windows.

10:30 AM

Visit Mehtab Bagh (Optional)

A riverside garden perfectly aligned with the Taj Mahal, ideal for peaceful reflection and photography.

11:30 AM

Explore Local Arts (Optional)

Stop at local handicraft stores for marble inlay work, leather goods, and souvenirs.

12:30 PM

Drive back to Delhi

Sit back and relax in your AC car for the return journey to Delhi.

4:00 PM

Drop-off at Delhi

End of the tour with lifelong memories. Drop-off at your Delhi hotel or airport.
